# Privacy

Hue analyzes your photo entirely on your device, in your browser.

- **No upload.** Your photo is never sent to any server.
- **No storage.** Nothing is saved after you close or refresh the page. There are no cookies, no analytics, no accounts.
- **No training.** Your photo is not used to train any model.
- **One external load.** The face-detection model (~3 MB, from Google's MediaPipe CDN) is downloaded once so analysis can run locally. Only the model file is fetched — your image is never part of any request.

Questions: open an issue on this repository.
